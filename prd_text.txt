SALON MANAGEMENT, BOOKING & MEMBERSHIP SYSTEM 
Complete System Overview 
 
INTRODUCTION 
1. CUSTOMER ENTRY (QR CODE / WEBSITE) 
When a customer enters the salon, they have three ways to begin: 
• They scan the QR code displayed in the salon, OR 
• They visit the website address printed under the QR code, OR. 
 
Homepage Features 
Once on the website, the customer lands on a clean homepage that includes: 
• Login / Sign Up button 
• "Book a Service" button 
• "Schedule Appointment" option 
• Wallet balance (for logged-in users only) 
• Promotional banners (flyers, discounts, offers) 
• Available services (Haircut, Braiding, Gaming, Snooker, etc.) 
• Announcements (e.g., "20% off this weekend") 
 
2. USER TYPES 
There are two types of users on the system: 
 
A. Guest Users (Not Logged In) 
Guests can: 
• Book services 
--- PAGE 2 ---
• Schedule appointments 
• Pay via cash or transfer 
Guests do NOT have access to: 
• A wallet 
• Membership benefits 
• Booking history 
 
B. Registered Users (Logged In) 
They can do everything a Guest user can do, but also have access to: 
• A personal wallet (deposit and spend) 
• Membership level (Silver, Gold, or VIP) 
• Full history of visits and payments 
• Exclusive membership benefits and discounts 
 
3. USER JOURNEY A — GUEST USER (Not Logged In) 
 
Step 1 — Landing Page 
The customer scans the QR code or visits the website. 
They land on the homepage which displays: 
• Login / Sign Up button 
• "Book a Service" button 
• Promotional flyers and banners 
• Current announcements and offers 
• List of available services 
The guest does not need to log in to book a service. 
 
--- PAGE 3 ---
Step 2 — Select Service 
The customer clicks "Book a Service". 
The system displays all available services: 
• Haircut 
• Braiding 
• Shaving 
• Gaming 
• Snooker 
• And any other services added by the owner 
The customer selects their preferred service and style (if applicable). 
The system immediately displays the fixed price. 
 
Step 3 — Choose Action 
The customer is given two options: 
• Proceed Now (walk-in, serve me today) 
• Schedule (book an appointment for a later date and time) 
 
Path A1 — Proceed Now 
The customer selects Proceed Now and moves to Select Payment Method: 
Option 1 — Transfer (Paystack) 
• Customer receives an account number 
• Transfers the exact amount 
• Payment is auto-confirmed by the system 
• Ticket is generated 
Option 2 — Cash 
• Customer selects cash 
--- PAGE 4 ---
• Ticket is generated immediately 
• Worker collects and confirms payment physically at the point of service 
 
Path A2 — Schedule (Book Appointment) 
The customer selects Schedule and fills in: 
• Preferred date 
• Preferred time 
• Preferred worker (optional) 
They then move to Select Payment Method: 
• Transfer (Paystack) 
• Cash (confirmed at arrival) 
After payment, the customer receives an Appointment Ticket containing: 
• Ticket ID 
• Date and time 
• Service booked 
• Assigned worker 
 
Step 4 — Ticket Generated 
Whether walk-in or scheduled, the guest receives a ticket with: 
• Unique Ticket ID 
• Service details 
• Worker assigned 
• Payment status (Paid / Pending) 
This ticket is shown to the worker before any service begins.

5. WORKER PROCESS 
--- PAGE 9 ---
 
Step 7 — Worker Verifies Ticket 
The customer shows their ticket code to the worker. 
The worker enters the code into the system and sees: 
• Customer name 
• Service requested 
• Fixed price 
• Payment status 
 
Step 8 — Cash Collection (If Applicable) 
If the payment method is cash: 
• The system displays the exact amount to collect 
• The worker collects the full payment 
• The worker clicks "Confirm Payment" 
The worker does not enter the price manually — the system records the correct amount 
automatically. 
 
Step 9 — Start Service 
The worker clicks "Start Service". 
The system updates the worker's status to Busy. 
 
Step 10 — Complete Service 
After finishing: 
• The worker clicks "Complete" 
• The service is recorded as done 
• The worker's status returns to Available 
--- PAGE 10 ---
 
Worker Dashboard Summary 
Workers can: 
• Enter and verify ticket codes 
• View full service details for each customer 
• Confirm cash payments 
• Start and complete services 
Worker statuses are either Available or Busy — the system uses this for auto-assignment.
